Ndewo! Ị na-eme nke ọma!

As a mom, I've always belived that the way to a child's heart is through a plate of nutritious and tasty meal. My journey into a healthy planning began when i had my son. Watching him grow and knowing that every meal i prepared nourished him filled me with purpose. Yet, as exciting as it is, I also struggled-like many parents-trying to find meals thats are both healthy and culturally rooted in our Nigerian values. This blog is my attempt to bring together the joy, the learning, and the fun of preparing meals that reflects who we are. I want to share with other parents the trick and ideas to balanced meals our children will love.

This platform is more than just a blog; it's a community where parents can interact, learn, and support one another as we journey through parenthood. Each child is a precious gift, and together we can create beautiful moments around the meals we serve them. Our kids deserve the best and together, we can ensure that every meal is a step towards a healthier, happier future. Let's share these cherished experience and inspire one another every step of the way.

Nri Ngbanwe-Flexible Meal Plan

While I do my best to plan a balanced meal schedule, I often interchange meals depending on availability and what my child prefers on certain days. Additionally, I make sure to serve fruits, fruit juices, fuits/vegetables puree and healthy snacks either in between meals or along with the main dishes. This approach allows for more flexibility and ensures my son is always excited about mealtime.

Parents can adapt this plan to suit their family's preferences, always ensuring a fun and nutritious eating experience.
